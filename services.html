Garment care, refined

Every fibre. Cared for.

A specialist process for every category — because cotton, silk, leather, and wool each ask for something different.

◐

Dry Cleaning

Solvent-based cleaning using SolvonK4 — a bio-based, biodegradable system that's gentler on natural fibres than perc or hydrocarbon. Ideal for wools, suits, silks, and tailored garments.

≋

Wash & Iron

Computer-controlled water-based washing with fabric-matched detergents and softeners. Crisply hand-pressed and folded or hung as you prefer.

⌐

Steam Press Only

For garments that just need finishing — professional steam pressing on form-fit mannequins that respect the original cut.

✦

Bridal & Heirloom

Hand-care line for wedding lehengas, sherwanis, and embellished pieces. Beadwork is individually inspected and reinforced where needed.

◇

Stain Specialist

Stain mapping at intake, classification by type (protein, oil, tannin, dye), and targeted treatment. We diagnose before we treat.

⬡

Curtains & Drapes

On-site take-down service for floor-length drapes, blinds, and large textiles. Returned ready to re-hang.

⌬

Leather & Suede

Re-conditioning, recolouring, and gentle solvent cleaning for jackets, bags, and leather upholstery covers.

❖

Monsoon Storage

Climate-controlled garment storage from June to October with monthly inspection. Ideal for woollens and sherwanis.

◈

Carpet & Rug Care

Deep extraction cleaning for wool, silk, and synthetic rugs. Pile-direction restoration and odour neutralisation.

⬢

Subscription Care

Monthly plans with weekly pickups — ideal for executives, families, and households that need consistent garment rotation.

◯

Shoe & Sneaker Care

Specialist cleaning, deodorising, and restoration for premium sneakers, loafers, and dress shoes.

◬

Repair & Alterations

Buttons, hems, seams, lining replacement — small repairs handled alongside cleaning, in-house.
