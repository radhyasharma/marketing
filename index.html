What we do

Specialised care
for every fabric.

From everyday shirts to bridal lehengas, each garment passes through a process tailored to its weave, dye, and history.

◐

Dry Cleaning

Solvent-based cleaning for wools, silks, and tailored garments. Gentle on fibres, ruthless on stains.

≋

Wash & Iron

Water-based cleaning with fabric-matched detergents, finished by hand for a crisp, ready-to-wear feel.

⌐

Steam Press

Professional steam pressing using form-fit equipment that follows the garment's intended silhouette.

✦

Bridal Care

Heirloom-grade cleaning for wedding lehengas, sherwanis, and embellished sarees. Hand-finished, individually inspected.

◇

Stain Removal

Specialist treatment for protein, oil, tannin, and dye stains. We diagnose before we treat.

⬡

Curtains & Linen

On-site take-down and re-hanging service for curtains, drapes, and large household textiles.

⌬

Leather & Suede

Re-conditioning, recolouring, and gentle cleaning for jackets, bags, and leather goods.

❖

Garment Storage

Climate-controlled monsoon storage with monthly inspection. Your woollens, perfectly preserved.
