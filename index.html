What we do

Specialised care
for every fabric.

From everyday shirts to bridal lehengas, each garment passes through a process tailored to its weave, dye, and history.

◐

Dry Cleaning

Licensed and certified dry cleaning for wools, silks, and tailored garments. Gentle on fibres, ruthless on stains.

✦

Wedding Suits & Gowns

Specialized care for wedding suits, gowns, sherwanis, and embellished pieces. Hand-finished, individually inspected.

◇

Silk & Delicate Fabrics

Expert handling of silk, chiffon, and delicate fabrics with processes tailored to each weave and dye.

⌬

Suede & Leather Care

Re-conditioning, recolouring, and gentle cleaning for jackets, bags, and leather goods.

⬡

Drapery & Curtains

Expert handling of drapery, curtains, and large household textiles. Returned ready to re-hang.

≋

Wash & Iron

Water-based cleaning with fabric-matched detergents, finished by hand for a crisp, ready-to-wear feel.

⌐

Steam Press

Professional steam pressing using form-fit equipment that follows the garment's intended silhouette.

❖

Stain Removal

Specialist treatment for protein, oil, tannin, and dye stains. We diagnose before we treat.
