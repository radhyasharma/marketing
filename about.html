Our story

Built on the floor,
not in a boardroom.

Lakshmi Sundaram opened the first LS storefront in 2008 in a small lane in Chennai with one idea — that dry cleaning could be honest, careful, and accessible.

Eighteen years later, we operate processing plants in three cities, serve customers across India, and clean over 50,000 garments a month. The principles haven't changed: every piece is treated like it belongs to family.

Today we're still independently owned. Still hand-finishing every garment. Still answering the phone ourselves.
