Our story

Built on the floor,
not in a boardroom.

The Lachman family opened the first LS storefront in 1984 in Seikh Sarai, New Delhi with one idea — that dry cleaning could be honest, careful, and accessible.

Over 39 years later, we continue serving Delhi as an independently owned business. Every garment is treated with the same care we would give our own.

Today we're still independently owned. Still hand-finishing every garment. Still answering the phone ourselves.
